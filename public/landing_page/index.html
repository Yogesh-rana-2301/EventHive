What is EventHive?

EventHive is an AI-powered, event-first social platform where users discover people around them based on real-time and planned events like tech meetups, sports, concerts, or networking sessions. We bring real, meaningful, in-person connections back into your life.

Why Choose EventHive?

Everything you need to discover, connect, and build meaningful relationships through events.

Create or Join Events

Create your own events or discover exciting happenings around you. From tech meetups to sports, find your community.

Discover Live Events

See what's happening right now in your area. Join spontaneous gatherings and meet like-minded people instantly.

Match by Interest & Location

Our AI matches you with people who share your interests and are nearby, making connections more meaningful.

Verified User Profiles

AI-powered verification ensures authentic connections. Meet real people with genuine interests, not fake profiles.

Event-Based Feed

No random content. Your feed shows only events and activities relevant to your interests and location.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about EventHive.

Is EventHive free to use?

Yes! EventHive is completely free to download and use. All core features are available to everyone at no cost.

How does the verification system work?

Our AI-powered verification system uses advanced technology to ensure users are authentic. You'll complete a simple verification process that confirms your identity.

Who can join EventHive?

EventHive is open to anyone aged 18 and above who wants to make meaningful connections through shared events and activities.

How do I stay safe while meeting new people?

EventHive helps you make safer connections by showing user verification status, event history, and reviews from other attendees.

Can I use EventHive in my city?

EventHive is available in major cities worldwide and expanding rapidly. Check the app store to see if we're available in your area.
